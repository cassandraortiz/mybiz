Cranberry Moscow Mule

This drink will be lifting your spirits all through those holiday deadlines.

  1. Ingredients: Vodka, Cranberry Juice Cocktail, Ginger Beer, Lime Juice, Lime Wedges, Fresh Cranberries, sprigs of rosemary(optional)
  2. Pour over ice: 1 part Vodka, 1 part Cranberry, 2 parts Ginger beer, and 1 tbsp Lime Juice
  3. Garnish with lime wedges, Cranberries and spring of rosemary

Hippie Juice

Now here's a drink that make you feel like everything is all Peace & Love. Knock a few back and you'll think you're in Woodstock instead of in front of your computer!

  1. Ingredients: Raspberry lemonade, Watermelon Vodka, Coconut Rum, Triple Sec
  2. Mix: 3 shots vodka, 1 shot both Rum and Triple Sec
  3. Pour over Ice and add fill the rest with Raspberry lemonade
